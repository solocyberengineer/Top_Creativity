Our Story

CARE BEARS was founded by 5 exceptional young leaders {NATHASHA KAPEYI,PINDA FORTUIN,VERONIQUE NAPPIE,REZAAR OSMAN,CARL JAMES,and lastly SIVUYISIWE PETER} with a vision to create the most huggable, lovable, and enchanting teddy bears the world has ever seen. With a deep love for craftsmanship and a commitment to quality, CARE BEARS set out on a journey to design and handcraft teddy bears that would capture the hearts of people around the globe.
